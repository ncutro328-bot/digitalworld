Happiness comes now and then,
We cannot be sure just when.
But when it's there, enjoy each hour
Because happiness has such power,
Joy to you it will bring
Even make someone else sing.
What peace of mind happiness can show
Making you and others glow,
Nurture it, make it last
Forget the troubles of the past.
Never fear that it will go
for it could always grow
and then tomorrow there it will be,
For happiness can set you free.
